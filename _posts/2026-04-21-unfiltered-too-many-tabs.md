### Welcome to the Chaos. Grab a Chip.

If you’ve ever walked into a room and forgotten why, but simultaneously solved a complex professional problem and noticed that the lightbulb is vibrating at exactly 60Hz—**you’re in the right place.**

I’m Mal. I’m a parent, a professional, and a proud owner of a brain that runs on high-octane "spicy" energy. For a long time, I tried to turn the volume down. I tried to "standardize" my wiring to fit into corporate boxes and "quiet" parenting circles. 

**That era is over. No apologies.**

---

### Tab 1: The Architecture of Different Wiring
Growing up "spicy" meant seeing the world in 4K while everyone else was watching a grainy VHS. I didn't just learn things; I consumed them. But I also struggled with the "boring" stuff. My backstory isn't a straight line—it’s a lightning bolt. It’s full of hyper-fixations that turned into high-level skills and "distractions" that turned into my most creative career moves.

### Tab 2: The Corporate Spice-Test
In the professional world, "Neurodivergent" is often treated as a HR box to check. Here, we treat it as a **competitive advantage**. I’ve navigated the grind by leaning into my ability to see patterns others miss. I’ve learned that when you stop masking and start "seasoning" your work with your actual personality, the results aren't just better—they’re explosive.

### Tab 3: Parenting with Extra Heat
Raising the next generation of spicy humans is the hardest, loudest, and most rewarding "tab" I have open. There is no manual for this. We are building the plane while flying it, often while being yelled at about the specific texture of a chicken nugget. This Hub is where I share the raw, unpolished reality of parenting with different wiring.

### Why "NeuroSpicy Salsa"?
Because life is better with a little kick. Because different wiring isn't a design flaw—it's the secret ingredient. 

I’m dropping the mask, keeping the tabs open, and leaning into the heat. I’m glad you’re here for the ride.
